Mini Chocolate Chip Cookies

These mini chocolate chip cookies are crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside, and perfect for a sweet snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the flour and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. Cream the butter, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine ingredients and fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 8–10 minutes and let cool.
